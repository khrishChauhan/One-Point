ONE POINT ARCHITECTURE STUDIO is a multidisciplinary
architecture, interior design, and Urban Design firm founded in
2018.
Kushagra & Neha Vaish are the Joint Principal Architects of One
Point Architecture Studio.
The member of the firm has works experience at multiple scales
and with various organizations right from private clients to
corporates & NGOs. This allows them to experiment and diversify
work: master planning, architectural & interior projects, such as
luxury villas, high end residences and offices, as well as
hospitality & institutional projects.
One Point Architecture Studio design process is to re-connect
architecture with nature, make maximum use of space,
natural/traditional materials, lighting to create the urban and
liveable spaces. They believe that architecture has a power to
influence lifestyle and society. Thus, firm strives to create design
that inspires, approaching each project, regardless of size & scale.
Their forte is attention towards details and reinvention of space.
Honesty to design, client satisfaction, and sustainability are the
driving force. They are dedicated to individual design approaches
for every project, and maintaining a balance between
functionality & aesthetics, context, climate, material, cost & time-
frame.
The Principals are registered architects with the Council of
Architecture.

MASTER PLANNING
IMAGINING THE WESTWIND 
AT SANAND
The brief for this project was to design a future
western extension to the city of Ahmedabad,
near the town of Sanand. The project was to be
implemented as a Town Planning scheme.
The design envisioned an urban extension that
would be merge wih the exising urban fabric of
Ahmedabad. The Sanand Ghuma Road, and
Sarkhej Sanand Road were to be the primary
rapid trasit lines, with the Sanand Shela Road,
that cut through the city would be the city main
city level artery connecting to Ahmedabad. This
extension would be anchored by a Corporate
Hub along the Sarkhej Road. On either side of
the DFC would be a logistics hub, which would
respond to the strong industrial force that
Sanand town presents.
